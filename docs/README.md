# DCT AI Innovation Portfolio

## Overview

The DCT AI Innovation Portfolio showcases AI-powered assistants, governance frameworks, operational models, and delivery enablement solutions developed to support onboarding, quality, governance, and delivery excellence across the DCT organization.

---

## Portfolio Mission

Transform DCT delivery knowledge, governance, and operational processes into reusable AI-powered capabilities that accelerate onboarding, improve delivery consistency, reduce dependency on tribal knowledge, and improve delivery quality.
