# Purpose

## Why This Platform Exists

The DCT Platform Delivery Model was created to transform static architecture documentation, delivery planning artifacts, governance decisions, and readiness tracking into a single interactive operational platform.

Traditional documentation existed across PowerPoint presentations, Word documents, Azure DevOps stories, meeting notes, and architecture diagrams.

This created knowledge fragmentation and limited visibility into delivery readiness.

The platform consolidates these artifacts into a navigable experience that enables stakeholders to understand architecture, governance, delivery status, and platform ownership.

## Intended Users

* Product Owners
* Business Analysts
* Architects
* Developers
* QA Teams
* Executive Leadership

## Business Problems Solved

* Documentation fragmentation
* Slow onboarding
* Lack of architecture visibility
* Governance ambiguity
* Delivery status inconsistency
* Manual reporting effort

## Business Value

* Faster onboarding
* Improved transparency
* Better governance visibility
* Executive reporting support
* Reusable delivery framework
* Knowledge preservation
