# Executive Buddy

## Overview

Executive Buddy is an AI-powered Executive Reporting and Portfolio Intelligence Assistant designed to provide leadership teams with real-time visibility into delivery performance, portfolio health, strategic initiatives, and organizational readiness.

---

## Purpose

Executive Buddy helps leaders make informed decisions by transforming delivery data, sprint metrics, portfolio status, risks, and roadmap information into executive-level insights and actionable recommendations.

---

## Status

**Current Status:** Planned

**Maturity:** Concept

**Target Release:** Future Innovation Portfolio

---

## Description

Executive Buddy serves as the executive layer of the DCT AI Innovation Portfolio. It provides a consolidated view of delivery performance, strategic initiatives, innovation progress, resource utilization, and organizational readiness.

The goal is to reduce manual reporting and provide leadership with timely, accurate, and actionable information.

---

## Core Capabilities

- Executive Dashboard Generation
- Portfolio Health Monitoring
- PI and Sprint Performance Reporting
- Strategic Initiative Tracking
- Resource Utilization Analysis
- Risk and Dependency Reporting
- Delivery Trend Analysis
- Capacity Forecasting
- Innovation Portfolio Reporting
- Leadership Briefing Generation
- KPI Monitoring
- Governance Insights

---

## Business Value

Executive Buddy enables leadership teams to quickly understand delivery performance, portfolio health, and strategic progress without requiring manual compilation of reports from multiple sources.

Benefits include:

- Improved executive visibility
- Faster decision-making
- Reduced reporting effort
- Better portfolio governance
- Increased delivery predictability
- Enhanced strategic alignment
